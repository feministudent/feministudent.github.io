where we work

We are a team of students working towards equal representation and opportunities for women and other minorities across all professions. We encourage all women to become educated on the status and success of women in their field and provide resources to do so through our website, including information on Title IX and sexual harrassment policies for universities across the United States. Through our outreach efforts, we stimulate new discussions on how we can level the playing field in every discipline.

the team

Equal representation of women and other minority groups in professional fields.

This includes women in higher paid and managerial positions.


A higher standard of education and greater opportunities for women and other minorities.


An end to hostile attitudes toward women and sexual harrassment in the workplace.

Greater accountability and appropriate punishment of perpetrators.

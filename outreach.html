FEMINISTUDENT

outreach

our initiatives to spark change in the world of academia and other professions target four groups:
high school, undergraduate, graduate, and career women.



undergrad, grad, & professionals: feministudent conference

Women from all walks of life can learn from each other. We are all feministudents.

The efforts of women to combat inequality and the obstacles they face in this fight are often unique to their discipline or career field; nevertheless, there exist similarities among these movements. We aim to bring together women students, faculty, and professionals from across the country to find common ground and discover barriers to women that exist in other specializations that are not their own. From this, we hope to inspire a more united conversation among students and professionals that leads to new perspectives and ideas on how to remedy gender disparity, both within their own field and across the board.

everyone: feministudent youtube channel

The first step toward righting society's wrongs against women and other minorities is talking about it.

Without actively trying to surround ourselves with a diversity of perspectives, we can often remain within a very homogeneous and isolated community. By bringing our discussions surrounding the plight of women and minorities in academia and the professions to the web, we hope to draw others into the conversation who wouldn't otherwise join in or consider points of view besides their own. Check in with us twice a month for interviews with female leaders in their field, 'a day in the life' segments, book and podcast reviews, and more.
